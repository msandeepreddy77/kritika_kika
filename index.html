FAQs

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

Are these prices for real?

Oh, absolutely not! We just threw in random numbers to confuse you. Of course, they're real! We're running a discount though, instead of 200, it's just 199. What a steal, right?

Do you offer any discounts?

Oh sure, we've got a special discount for people who can recite Shakespeare backwards while hopping on one foot. Just kidding! We believe in the value of art, so no discounts, unless you're best friends with the Mona Lisa.

Can I get it cheaper elsewhere?

Absolutely, just head down to your local discount superstore and see if they've got any Picassos on aisle 5. But hey, good luck finding art with soul and meaning next to the frozen peas.

Is shipping free?

Oh, of course! We have a fleet of magical unicorns that deliver your art for free, right to your doorstep. Just kidding! Shipping is like the seasoning on a fine dish - it's extra, but oh so worth it when you taste it.
